About Us

  • The Quiz Club of MIT is a club dedicated to building a passionate quizzing community within MIT, encouraging exploration beyond academics, and enhancing critical thinking, communication, and teamwork through quizzes.

  • We organizes competitions that foster both collaboration and competition, represents MIT in inter-collegiate events, and expands the quizzing culture by conducting open events in educational institutions around MIT.
